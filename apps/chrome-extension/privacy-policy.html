Privacy Policy

QarbonQuery Carbon Tracker

Your Privacy Matters: QarbonQuery is designed with privacy first. We collect minimal data necessary for functionality and never share personal information with third parties.

1. Data We Collect

QarbonQuery collects only the following data to provide carbon footprint tracking:

  • Website URLs: The web addresses you visit to calculate emissions for specific sites
  • Timestamps: When you visit websites to track usage patterns over time
  • Carbon Emissions Estimates: Calculated values based on your browsing activity

2. How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Chrome Extension Local Storage: Your data never leaves your browser
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store any of your data on external servers
  • No Account Required: No registration or personal information needed

3. Data Retention

Your data is retained according to the following policy:

  • Automatic Cleanup: Older data is periodically removed to prevent excessive storage use
  • User Control: You can clear all data at any time through the extension settings
  • Uninstall Cleanup: All data is automatically removed when you uninstall the extension

4. Personal Data

No Personal Data Collection: QarbonQuery does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or personal details.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We maintain strict privacy standards:

  • Zero Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared with external services, advertisers, or analytics platforms
  • No Data Sales: We never sell or monetize your data
  • Local Processing Only: All calculations and processing happen locally in your browser

6. User Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear All Data: Use the "Clear Data" button in extension settings to remove all stored information
  • Disable Tracking: Temporarily pause tracking through the extension popup
  • Selective Deletion: Remove specific site data through the detailed view
  • Export Data: View and export your carbon footprint data for personal use

7. Security

We implement security best practices:

  • Local Storage Encryption: Data is stored securely within Chrome's protected storage system
  • No Network Transmission: Your browsing data never leaves your device
  • Minimal Permissions: The extension requests only necessary permissions for functionality

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify users of significant changes through the extension interface. Continued use of the extension after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

9. Children's Privacy

QarbonQuery is not specifically designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@qarbonquery.com
  • GitHub: https://github.com/alex-delvision/qarbon-query
  • Issues: Report issues or privacy concerns
